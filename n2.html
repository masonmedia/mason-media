N2 Speech Therapy

N2 Speech Therapy is a Toronto Speech-Language Pathology services provider. They offer a broad range of one-on-one treatment options for everything from autism spectrum disorders to aphasia and post-stroke and brain injury rehabilitation. Drawing on diverse experience, and making use of the latest treatment strategies and technological aids, N2 treats a wide range of clients, from children to adults.

Design, specs, approach

N2 required a complete brand design including website, logo, banner images, background photography, and copywriting. Requirements included a bold, professional layout, clean presentation of company information, services, rates, and contact information, and a blog for updatable content and SEO. The design solution consisted of an animated, parallaxing front page with content synopses, custom banner and footer images, logo, icons, and social sharing buttons. The color scheme and images were selected and created with a combined focus on originality and industry-consistent themes.

*Tech specs: HTML5, CSS3, Wordpress, Yoast SEO, Copywriting, Graphic Design.
